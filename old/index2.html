What Does High Quality Care Look Like?

ShatterProof has developed 7 national principles of high-quality addiction treatment providers:

  • Does this facility offer same day access/walk-in appointments for patient’s requiring outpatient care?
  • If this facility has no available beds, will this provider help patients find immediate alternative treatment?
