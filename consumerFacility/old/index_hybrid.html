What Does High Quality Care Look Like?


ShatterProof has developed 7 national principles of high-quality addiction treatment providers:

  • Addiction alters brain chemistry. You need to have access to treatment as soon as you are ready.

One size does NOT fit all. Treatment must consider your unique needs—with frequent check-ins and adjustments from your provider.

Unless your substance use disorder is severe, inpatient treatment may not be the best option for you. Long-term outpatient care is the key to recovery for everyone.

Many people with addiction also suffer from other mental or physical illnesses. Your treatment plan should include treatment for ALL of your illnesses.

Behavioral health services help individuals manage their disease and sustain recovery. These services should be offered by properly trained and accredited providers.

Just like with any other chronic disease, medication is appropriate for treating some addictions. You should have easy access to medication if you need it.

You will need emotional and practical support from your family members, peers, and community on your journey to recovery.
